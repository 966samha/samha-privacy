Account & Data Deletion Request - Samha

This page explains how users of the Samha application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to privacy@samhaapp.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email and phone number
  4. Our team will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Booking history
  • Messages and reviews
  • Stored preferences

What Data May Be Retained

  • Financial transaction records retained for 5–10 years as required by Saudi law
  • Data required for legal compliance or fraud prevention

Partial Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of specific data without deleting their account by contacting privacy@samhaapp.com.
