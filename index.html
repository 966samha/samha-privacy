Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Samha ("Samha," "Company," "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, store, disclose, and protect personal data when you access or use the Samha mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and applicable international data protection standards.

By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Samha application. It does not apply to third-party services that may be linked within the app. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Booking details and appointment history
  • Beauty preferences
  • Optional profile photos
  • Messages, reviews, and customer support communications

3.2 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through certified third-party payment providers, including EDFAPAY. We do not store or process full credit or debit card numbers on our servers.

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Log data (access times, pages viewed, actions taken)
  • App usage analytics
  • Location data (with your explicit permission, for displaying nearby salons)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on one or more of the following lawful grounds:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., booking services)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests, provided such interests do not override your rights

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Facilitate bookings between users and salons
  • Process transactions
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • With Partner Salons: Necessary booking information to enable appointment fulfillment.
  • With Service Providers: Payment processors, cloud hosting providers, analytics providers, and support platforms under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Saudi laws, regulations, or court orders.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset sale.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular system monitoring and updates
  • Secure data centers compliant with Saudi regulatory requirements

While we strive to protect your data, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations (including financial record retention requirements under Saudi law, typically 5–10 years), resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

When personal data is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights Under PDPL

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability where applicable

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@samhaapp.com. We may require identity verification before processing certain requests.

10. International Data Transfers

Personal data is primarily stored within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. If data is transferred internationally, such transfers will comply with PDPL requirements and include appropriate safeguards.

11. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain functionality, remember preferences, analyze usage, and improve performance. Users may manage certain tracking preferences through device or browser settings.
